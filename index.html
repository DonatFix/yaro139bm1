About Re-Wrap Custom Upholstery in Toronto

Based in Toronto and serving clients for over 16 years in the GTA, Re-Wrap Custom Upholstery specializes in professional furniture restoration and custom upholstery services. Our services provide homeowners with a timely, cost-effective alternative to replacing worn out furniture or damaged antiques, as well as a way to personalize the furniture for any room. We pride ourselves on providing luxury service and elegant solutions at competitive prices that keep our customers coming back to us for more.



Schedule your furniture restoration or custom upholstery appointment today by calling Re-Wrap Custom Upholstery.
